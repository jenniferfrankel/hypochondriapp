HypochondriApp is a symptom tracker that lets you keep track of all kinds of symptoms (for example Fever, Coughing, Sleepiness etc.) in a simple way. Just one click adds a symptom event to your symptom history. Or you can add more detail by choosing severity level and duration of your symptom event. And you can add a comment as well. Of course you can go back and edit each symptom event at any time.
