Version 0.1
January 16, 2013

The first Beta of symptom tracker HypochondriApp is here! Try it out and let us know what you think.

  • HypochondriApp currently only supports iPhones, and has been tested with iOS 5 & 6
  • Features: Create symptoms, add/edit symptom history events, and view symptom history in graph form (landscape view).
  • Known issue: Wonky scrolling.
