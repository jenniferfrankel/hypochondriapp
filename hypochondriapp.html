Introducing HypochondriApp

HypochondrioApp is the Symptom Tracker to end all symptom history confusion. Easily track and edit symptom events. Have your symptom history available whenever and wherever you need it. Make informed decisions about your health based on facts and accurate symptom histories.

Have you ever tried to remember when a certain symptom started ("was it weeks, months or even years ago?"), or wished that you had kept better track of your migraine attacs when discussing them with your doctor? A documented history of symptoms can help you discover important patterns ("splitting head aches every Sunday morning? huh, that's interesting!"), and make healthy life choices. But keeping long detailed lists of symptom histories, and finding them when they are needed, just seems like soo much work. Nobody is that organized.

And you don't have to be! With the HypochondriApp Symptom Tracker you get all the benefits of being super organized, without the effort! HypochondriApp lets you keep track of all your symptoms in a simple and straightforward way. Customize the list of symptoms to fit your needs, and start tracking. With just one click you can add an event to your symptom history, or if you want more detail you can specify severity and duration of the symptom event. E.g. "Nausea, 7 on a scale of 1-10, duration: 2h, comment: 'started right after I finished my 6th chocolate peanut butter cupcake, I hope it's not food poisoning...'"

Try it now! Go to: jennifer.frankel.nu/hypoapp in your iPhone or iPod Touch web browser (support for other devices is not yet available, but coming soon!). Or, if you'd rather not type so much, go to: tinyurl.com/hypapp and start tracking!

The 1-minute tour

Symptoms

Your list of symptoms, all in one place.

Symptom history

Each symptom has its own history list. Scroll down to check how long this has been going on, and how frequently it occurs.

Customize

Create the symptoms you wish to keep track of, and add them to your list.

Visualize

Turn your phone sideways to view your symptom history in a graph. Discover interesting patterns.

Track

Add a new symptom event to your history. Just one click required!
